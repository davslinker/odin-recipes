Kraft Macaroni and Cheese dinner


This canadian classis, loved through the ages, is criminally mislabeled on the box.
Here, I will show you the best way to make this dish.

'If music be the food of love, play on' - Twelfth night
If this quote can be taken to mean that food is music, then KD has to be the trumpet
blast from a Reel big fish song. Not everyone loves it. I do.

Ingredients

  • Box of Kraft Dinner
  • Butter (or failing that, margarine)
  • Milk (whole milk works best!)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. The exact amount is unimportant,
    but you need enough to comfortable submerge the mac.
  2. Once the water is brought to a boil, add the dry macaroni noodles. Stir occasionally.
  3. Remove the noodles from the water and drain after 5 minutes only.
    The box may say seven, but it is lying to you. Drain most of the water,
    but not all.
  4. Mix cheese powder from box, milk, and butter together with noodles until all is
    an ungodly orange mess of bullshit. Add additional flavor enhancers, such as salt, pepper,
    additional cheeses, etc. But NEVER ADD KETCHUP.

That's it! You've made a wonderful dish! Good job!
