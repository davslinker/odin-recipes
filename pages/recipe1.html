Sausages on toast


Just like it says on the tin, this is a simple recipe, but delicious.

When you have a young child in the house, things can get frantic.
Sometimes little things like having a nice breakfast at your own pace, well you don't get that luxury.
This is one of my favourite dishes to make for when i just don't have time to make something lavish but still want a shot of flavour in the mouth.

You should be able to make it with most of the things you have in your house already!

Ingredients:

  • Sausages
  • bread (the thicker the better)
  • butter
  • mustard
  • your hot sauce of choice (I like something with chipotle!)

Steps:

  1. Spread a generous amount of butter on a piece of toast.
  2. Cut your sausages into little slices and put on a plate.
  3. Microwave the sausages on high for a minute or so, enough to get them spectacularly hot.
  4. Straight from the microwave, put the sausages on the buttery bread. Marvel as the heat melts the butter.
  5. Top with hit sauce and mustard. Plate optional, depending on level of degen you are.

And that's it! You're now enjoying a wonderful dish worthy of a community colege student.
